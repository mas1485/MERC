What does it mean to be a Captain?

  • Captains lead our groups. They organize training runs and drive our members to meet their goals.

  • Captains are expected to plan routes, work with members of the group to come up with their own schedule, proactively engage with our members to participate in events and ensure that the group runs are safe.

  • There are many qualities to becoming a captain but we fundamentally our captains are engaging, passionate and are good communicators.

  • Want to gain leadership experience? Want to have a more active role in the club? Captaincy is a perfect opportunity to help our members fulfill their goals.

  • You can apply for captaincy with a simple click! Whether you have run with us before or you are a new member.

  • You will get the freedom to run your team how you like, whether you want a casual group or you train for marathons, captaincy is what you make of it!

  • Check out our club captain policy in the legal section for more information on the roles and responsibilities of our captains.

Policies

Lead a group

Looking to gain leadership experience? Become a captain and get the references you need from the club and the people you inspire.

Run your way

Our captains are given freedom to host their own schedules and organize the groups the way they want to.

Promote your ideas

We want to hear how we can improve. We want our captains to be proactive and help us so we can help our communities.

Promotions

As we expand we will be looking for individuals to help us take on key roles and responsibilities. Our captains will be able to showcase that they have what it takes for the next step.
