Who are we?

    We are Multi Ethnic Running Club CIC, also known as MERC. We are a running club that currently operates in Southend, Essex but we are looking to expand into other areas of Essex and the UK.

    We have a simple vision, to improve our communities and support the people in them.

    Our motto is VICTORY THROUGH DIVERSITY, we strive to create a club in which all members feel welcome and are proud to be a part of.

    We raise funds for charities and projects in our local areas to support people in need and benefit our local community.

    As a running club we provide a space for members to engage in fitness and social activities.

    We are a not-for-profit entity which is made up entirely of volunteers, our financial strategy is set to ensure that any use of our funds benefits the community, is used for charitable purposes or improves the services that we provide for our members.

    Want to see what being a member is all about? Check out the membership page.

    Want to know more about us? Get in contact with us to learn more.
