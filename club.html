Who are we?

    We are Multi Ethnic Running Club CIC, also known as MERC.

    We have a simple vision, to improve our communities and support the people in them.

    Our motto is VICTORY THROUGH DIVERSITY, we strive to create a club in which all members feel welcome and are proud to be a part of.

    We raise funds for charities and projects in our local areas to support people in need and benefit our local community.

    As a running club we provide a space for members to engage in fitness and social activities.
