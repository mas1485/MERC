What does it mean to be a member?

    Our members are the heartbeat of our organisation, they drive our projects, engage with our community and run our club!

    Looking for a casual run in the day with your friends? Training for a marathon? Simply want to raise money for charity? Our members enjoy the benefits of our club and we work with them to acheive their goals.

    We accept anyone 18 and over as a member of the club, ofcourse we do have events that include children. Under 18's do not need to become members to run with us, they only need a adult member to be a guardian to look after their needs.

    Our yearly membership is £20.00, giving you access to our club activities, race events and social gatherings.

Policies

Feel Healthy

Running is a great way to excercise as it improves your cardiovascular health, keeping our members people fit and strong.

Our members help people by supporting us, their runs are a small part of how we strive to better our communities.

Get social

Our members socialize with friends whilst on their runs and engage with people across the community, learning to communicate and lead groups.
